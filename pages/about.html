Our Story

Built on transparency

We started Glass Co. because we were tired of software agencies that worked in black boxes. We believe your software partners should be as clear as your code.

Glass Co. was founded in 2017 by a team of engineers who had spent years inside agencies that over-promised and under-delivered. The name isn't just a logo — it's a commitment. Everything we do is visible to you.

You'll have access to our project management boards, staging environments, and weekly written updates. We don't hide behind jargon. If something is hard, we'll tell you it's hard. If something will take longer, we'll tell you before it's a problem.

Today, we're a team of 40+ engineers, designers, and product thinkers working with startups and scale-ups across fintech, healthtech, logistics, and enterprise SaaS.

Our values

Craft Over Speed

We move fast, but never at the cost of quality. Technical debt is a tax we refuse to pass to you.

People First

Software is built by humans for humans. We care about the wellbeing of our team as much as our products.
